About AHP

Design, data, and fun drive product development.

Eoghan McHugh, a cycling and guitar enthusiast, started ACP in 2023 and AGP in 2024 to create giftware for cyclists and guitarists.

Each product answers four key questions:

  1. Is it clear to the customer what the product is about?
  2. Is the design and quality gift-worthy?
  3. Will the recipient enjoy the product?
  4. Will the recipient get something from their gift?
Every item is crafted with these principles, ensuring thoughtful, well-designed gifts for cyclists and guitarists alike.
